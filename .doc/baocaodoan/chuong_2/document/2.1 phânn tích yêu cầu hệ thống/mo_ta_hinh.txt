Hinh 2.1.1. So do phan ra chuc nang cua he thong Taskify

Goi y mo ta: Hinh nay mo ta cac nhom chuc nang chinh cua Taskify duoc trien khai trong source code, bao gom xac thuc, quan ly task, ghi chu, tai chinh, focus, chat AI, cau hinh AI va quan tri nguoi dung.
